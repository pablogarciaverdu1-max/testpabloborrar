Slide 02
Advanced Routing & Continuous Optimization Logic
Intelligent tiering ensures the right BDR touches the right account at the right time.
Tier 1
Strategic Accounts
Criteria
  • Perfect Ideal Customer Profile match
  • High Annual Recurring Revenue potential
  • Strategic brands
Routing
  • Senior BDR assigned
  • Manual research required
  • Personalized outreach sequence
Tier 2
Growth Accounts
Criteria
  • Good Ideal Customer Profile fit
  • Medium opportunity value
Routing
  • Semi-personalized sequences
  • AI-generated first-touch messaging
Tier 3
Volume Accounts
Criteria
  • Low fit
  • Unclear Ideal Customer Profile fit
Routing
  • Fully automated outbound campaigns
Dynamic Routing Logic
IF
  Industry = Retail / Apparel / Sporting Goods
  AND Title contains:
    CEO  ·  Category Manager  ·  Sales Director

THEN Assign Priority Score

  High Score    → Tier 1
  Medium Score → Tier 2
  Low Score    → Tier 3
Continuous Optimization Loop
Weekly Review Metrics
  • Reply rate
  • Meeting rate
  • SQL rate
  • Opportunity creation rate
Optimization Actions
  • Adjust scoring thresholds
  • Update Ideal Customer Profile filters
  • Promote high-performing segments
  • Remove underperforming segments
Outcome
BDRs spend time only on accounts most likely to convert.
